About Me

Hi, I’m Irfan! I believe pets make us better humans—and I’ve dedicated my work to capturing the moments that show why. As a lifelong animal lover and experienced pet caretaker, I bring a deep understanding of animal behavior to every photo session, allowing me to create images that are personal, playful, and full of heart.

I work primarily with natural light and love photographing pets in familiar environments where they can truly be themselves—whether that’s at home, at the park, or nestled in your arms. My sessions are relaxed, play-focused, and always centered on making your pet feel safe, comfortable, and seen.

These aren’t just photos—they’re lasting memories of the bond you share. If you want to capture your pet’s unique personality, I’m here to help tell that story through images you’ll cherish for years to come.
